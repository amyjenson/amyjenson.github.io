I am second-year MSc student in the Department of Mathematical Sciences at Montana State University. I'm working with Scott McCalla and Mark Skidmore on a MSc thesis modeling brine flow through ice-walled channels.

My research interests lie in subglacial hydrology and modeling different aspects of drainage such as outburst floods and channel evolution. I am interested in learning more about ice-ocean interactions as well as expanding my knowledge of ice dynamics.

I am interested in using my mathematical background in research applications such as geophyics, hydrology, and glaciology.

My goal is to be enrolled in a PhD program in applied math or geophysics in Fall 2022 or Fall 2023.
