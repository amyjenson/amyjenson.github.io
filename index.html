Amy J. Jenson

I am second-year M.S. student in the Department of Mathematical Sciences at Montana State University. I'm working with Dr. Scott McCalla and Dr. Mark Skidmore on a M.S. thesis modeling brine flow through ice-walled channels.

My research interests lie in subglacial hydrology and modeling different aspects of drainage such as outburst floods and channel evolution. I am also interested in learning more about ice-ocean interactions as well as expanding my knowledge of ice dynamics.

I plan to contintue using my mathematical background in research applications such as geophyics, hydrology, and glaciology.

I am currently applying to PhD programs with the intent of being enrolled in Fall 2022.
